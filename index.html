Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Tacos are believed to have first appeared in the United States in the 19th century, brought by Mexican immigrants to the southwestern regions of the country. The exact timeframe is not precisely documented, but it is generally accepted that tacos gained popularity in the U.S. during the late 19 and early 20th centuries.
